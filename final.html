CSC 106 Final Project

The final project that I coded is a science quiz app. This app was designed with
middle school or early high school students in mind as the target demographic.
I wanted to create this app because I am a Secondary Education and Biology major.
I was interested to see if I could create a project that might help me in the future in
my own classrooms. There are 3 different difficulty levels of this quiz. I hope you
enjoy and tap into some of your old science knowledge to try and get a perfect score!
